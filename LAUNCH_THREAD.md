1️⃣ Input your team's AI subscriptions (tools, plans, seats)
2️⃣ Our engine cross-references for overlap (e.g. Copilot + Cursor) and idle tiers
3️⃣ Get a personalized shareable report with exact monthly/annual savings and action items
