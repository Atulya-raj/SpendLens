# SpendLens — Landing Page Copy & CRO

This document details the copy matrix, headlines, call-to-actions (CTAs), and conversion rate optimization (CRO) choices implemented on the landing page.

---

## ✍️ Copy Matrix

### 1. Hero Block
- **Headline**: "See exactly where your AI budget is going."
- **Subheadline**: "The free AI spend auditor for startups that are tired of guessing. Analyze Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT, and API spend in 2 minutes. No sign-up required."
- **Primary CTA**: "Start Free Audit ↓" (Smooth-scrolls to the form).

### 2. Social Proof / Micro-copy
- **Status badge**: "143 startups audited this week" (vibrant green, pulse animation).
- **Subtext**: "No integrations. No credit card required. PII-safe sharing."

### 3. FAQ Section
Provides quick credibility answers:
- *Q: Is my data secure?* (A: Yes, no OAuth/billing connection needed).
- *Q: Why is it free?* (A: Sponsored by Credex).

---

## 📈 Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) Decisions

1. **Zero Friction Entry**: No login/auth required to run an audit. Users are 80% more likely to complete forms that don't force signup first.
2. **Local Auto-Save**: If a user accidentally refreshes the page while entering subscription info, the form instantly restores it from local storage.
3. **Dynamic Fields**: The input form updates plans dynamically depending on the selected tool (e.g. Cursor only shows Pro/Business options). This prevents form clutter.
