## FAQ — 5 Real Q&As

**Q: Is my data private? I don't want to connect my billing accounts.**

A: You don't connect anything. There are no OAuth flows, no API integrations, and no billing access. You just type in what you're spending — the same thing you'd type into a notes app. We strip your email and company name before saving the public report, so even the shareable link contains zero personally identifiable info.

---

**Q: Why is this free?**

A: SpendLens is built and sponsored by [Credex](https://credex.rocks/), a platform that helps startups manage their SaaS spend. If your audit reveals significant savings, you'll see an optional offer from Credex to help you act on it. You're never forced into anything — the audit is fully free and always will be.

---

**Q: Does this actually work, or is it just generic advice?**

A: Every recommendation traces back to real vendor pricing as of May 2026. The audit engine checks for specific things: are you paying for Cursor *and* GitHub Copilot (redundant)? Are you on a Business plan with a team small enough that Pro would cover everything? Is your Anthropic API spend higher than equivalent Claude Team seats would cost? These are real checks, not vibes.

---

**Q: What AI tools do you support?**

A: Right now: Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Windsurf, OpenAI API, and Anthropic API. We cover the tools that show up most often in startup engineering stacks. More are coming — the rule engine is modular, so adding a new tool is straightforward.

---

**Q: Can I share my report with my team or CEO?**

A: Yes, that's the whole point. Every completed audit generates a permanent public link you can copy and paste into Slack or email. It shows the full breakdown — which tools, what you're overpaying, how much you'd save — without exposing your email or company name. You can also tweet it directly from the results page.
